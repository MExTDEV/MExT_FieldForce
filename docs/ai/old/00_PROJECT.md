# MExT FieldForce

## Project Mission
MExT FieldForce is the central platform for all field-related activities within M.Ex.T. Belgium.
The project was originally initiated to digitalise the coaching process between sales representatives and their sales managers. The objective was to replace paper-based coaching forms with a structured digital solution that allows coaching sessions to be planned, executed, evaluated and followed up through measurable action points and performance tracking.
During the project's evolution, the strategic vision expanded considerably.
Rather than developing multiple standalone applications, MExT FieldForce is becoming the single integrated platform for all operational tools used by field sales representatives. Existing and future applications such as Salesday, contract calculation, service modules and other field-related functionality will gradually be integrated into this platform.
Although the long-term vision covers the complete field organisation, the current development phase is fully focused on delivering a best-in-class digital coaching solution. All new development priorities currently support the coaching workflow.

## Current Development Focus
The long-term platform consists of multiple modules.
However, at this moment almost all development effort is focused on the Coaching module.
When implementing new functionality, improvements to the Coaching module have priority over future modules unless explicitly requested.

## Functional Ownership
The functional ownership of MExT FieldForce belongs to the business.
Primary business owner:
- External Sales Manager
Functional requirements are defined in collaboration with the Country Managers, ensuring that the application supports the operational needs of all participating countries.

## Technical Ownership
The application is designed and developed by the internal IT department.
The IT team is not merely responsible for implementing requested features. It actively contributes to improving business processes by proposing better workflows, identifying optimisation opportunities and designing solutions that go beyond the original functional request whenever they add measurable value.
Innovation and continuous improvement are considered an integral part of the development process.

## Development Philosophy
The objective is not to digitally reproduce existing paper processes.
Every feature should be evaluated critically.
Whenever possible, workflows should be simplified, automated and improved while preserving the business objectives.
Artificial Intelligence should actively propose improvements instead of only implementing requested functionality.
Suggestions for improving usability, reducing user actions or increasing automation are encouraged, provided they remain compatible with the project's architecture and business requirements.

## Target Users
MExT FieldForce is primarily developed as the daily operational tool for M.Ex.T.'s field sales organisation.
The application is intended for users who spend the majority of their working day visiting customers and therefore require fast, reliable and intuitive access to information while travelling.

### Expected User Base
Belgium
- Approximately 40 users
The Netherlands
- Approximately 30 users
Germany
- Approximately 40 users
Group Management
- Approximately 5 users with cross-country responsibilities

The expected production environment therefore consists of approximately 115 active users.

### User Profile
The majority of users are commercial field representatives.
Typical characteristics:
- Non-technical users
- Daily customer visits
- Frequent travelling
- Limited time for administration
- Need for immediate access to customer information
- Need for rapid data entry
The application should therefore minimise the number of clicks required to complete common tasks.

### Devices
Current platform
- Windows tablets

Future roadmap
The organisation intends to migrate towards Android tablets or Apple iPads around the beginning of 2028.
All newly developed functionality should therefore remain platform independent.
The application should never rely on Windows-specific functionality.

### Connectivity
Field users typically work through a mobile 5G internet connection.
Although connectivity is generally good, users frequently operate:
- inside industrial buildings
- in warehouses
- in technical rooms
- in remote locations

The application should therefore minimise network traffic and be prepared for temporary connectivity interruptions.

Offline capabilities remain an important long-term objective of the platform.

## Business Objectives
The primary objective of MExT FieldForce is not to digitise paper forms.
Its objective is to eliminate administrative work wherever possible, allowing field employees and sales managers to spend more time on activities that create value for customers.
Every new feature should contribute to one or more of the following objectives:
- Reduce administrative workload.
- Reduce duplicate data entry.
- Automate repetitive tasks.
- Increase coaching quality.
- Improve follow-up.
- Increase data quality.
- Provide immediate insights into performance.

The application should continuously replace manual processes with structured digital workflows.

### Example: Coaching Process
Current situation
A sales manager performs a coaching session using paper forms.
After the coaching session:
- paper forms must be processed manually;
- scores are calculated manually;
- results must be digitised;
- action points are followed up manually;
- representatives sign documents separately;
- monitoring the progress of action points is difficult.

Future situation
The complete coaching workflow is digital.
The application should:
- guide the sales manager through the coaching session;
- calculate all scores automatically;
- generate performance insights immediately;
- automatically create and manage action points;
- allow the representative to digitally approve the coaching;
- automatically monitor the follow-up of agreed actions;
- maintain the complete coaching history.

The objective is not simply to replace paper with a screen, but to redesign the entire workflow to become faster, more reliable and easier to manage.

## Product Vision

MExT FieldForce is intended to become the single operational platform for all field employees within the M.Ex.T. Group.

The long-term objective is to replace multiple standalone tools with one integrated platform covering coaching, sales, contracts, service and future field-related functionality.

The platform should continuously evolve without requiring users to switch between different applications.

## Why MExT FieldForce Exists
MExT FieldForce was deliberately developed as a custom software platform instead of adopting an existing CRM, coaching or field sales solution.
The operational processes within M.Ex.T. are highly specialised and have evolved over many years. During the evaluation phase, several commercial software packages were analysed and tested.
Although many products offered useful functionality, none of them matched the operational requirements of M.Ex.T.

Common findings included:
- Features that were unnecessary for M.Ex.T.
- Missing functionality that was considered essential.
- Workflows that did not match the daily reality of field representatives.
- Limited flexibility to adapt business processes.
- Complex licensing models for functionality that would never be used.

Rather than adapting the business to fit existing software, M.Ex.T. decided to build software that perfectly supports its own business processes.

## One Integrated Platform
Historically, field representatives had to work with multiple independent applications.
The vision of MExT FieldForce is to consolidate these separate tools into one integrated platform.
This provides several advantages:
- One login
- One consistent user experience
- One central database
- One security model
- One reporting platform
- One roadmap
- Reduced training requirements
- Faster implementation of new functionality

The objective is not only technical integration, but also operational simplification for the end user.

Every workflow inside MExT FieldForce is designed around the actual daily work of M.Ex.T. employees.

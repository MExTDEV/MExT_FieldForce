# Salestrainingen

## Status

Workflow status: `UNDEFINED`

## Known Purpose

Salestraining is intended as a structured training intervention for one or more field employees.

The detailed workflow has not yet been approved.

---

# Do Not Implement Without Business Definition

Do not invent:

- training creation form;
- participant selection;
- individual or group rules;
- agenda;
- attendance;
- training material;
- notification;
- report;
- evaluation;
- completion;
- approval;
- Planning behaviour;
- Action Points;
- reporting formulas.

---

# Decisions Required

- who may create and manage a Salestraining;
- countries, teams and users that may participate;
- single or multiple sessions;
- trainers;
- objectives and content;
- prior notification;
- attendance tracking;
- documents and photos;
- report and evaluation;
- Action Point creation;
- completion and cancellation;
- Hulpaanvraag relation;
- history and reporting;
- permissions and scope.

A Hulpaanvraag may record that a topic will be included in a future Salestraining without creating an undefined Salestraining record.

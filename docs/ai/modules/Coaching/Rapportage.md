# Rapportage

## Status

Functional area status: `UNDEFINED`

## Known Purpose

Rapportage is intended to provide Coaching history, performance insight, KPI analysis, Action Point follow-up and management reporting.

The final reports, formulas and access model are not yet approved.

---

# Existing Inputs

Potential existing inputs include:

- Completed Begeleidingen;
- Coaching scores;
- Performance Circle;
- KPI definitions and snapshots;
- Action Points;
- Contactmoment history;
- Hulpaanvraag outcomes;
- teams, countries and users.

The existence of data does not define a report.

---

# Do Not Invent

Do not invent:

- KPI formulas;
- score thresholds;
- management dashboards;
- report names;
- export contents;
- country comparisons;
- rankings;
- access rights;
- historical cut-off rules;
- inclusion of incomplete records;
- aggregation periods.

---

# Decisions Required

- report catalogue;
- intended audience;
- effective scope;
- source entities;
- inclusion and exclusion rules;
- formulas;
- periods;
- thresholds;
- comparisons;
- exports;
- privacy and audit requirements;
- performance requirements.

Completed Begeleidingen may feed reporting according to existing documented behaviour, but no new report or formula should be created without approval.
